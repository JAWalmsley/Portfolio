Users can scan a barcode on product found in a store to receive a list of its ingredients with a high carbon footprint and get a grade based on its eco-friendliness. The website retrieves food information from OpenFoodFacts and stores data in decentralized IPFS storage.



##### Winner of Deltahacks 9: Best Environmental Hack and Most Creative Use of Verbwire API

## Built with
* ReactJS
* Verbwire
* IPFS storage
* OpenFoodFacts API
